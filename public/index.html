Trump Twitter Grapher Alpha

Step 1: Sign In to Twitter

Why does this app need me to sign in to Twitter?

The Twitter API has extremely strict limits on what (free) apps can do/access, and how quickly. As a way of distributing this process, I'm asking you to sign in so the limit for how much you can refresh this app is based on your accout, not one app account. Don't worry, it can't post anything on your behalf.

Step 2: Sort and Graph Tweets

Below you can sort and graph tweets in specific "topics" that are determined by Regular Expressions. There are a few preprogrammed ones (see below for more info), and you can add your own as well. Make sure to click "sort tweets" after you add a new topic filter.

About this App

This app was inspired by this tweet by Hank Green.

There should be something that graphs the number of likes and RTs Trump gets by topic. Seems to be dropping. AHCA tweets do not do well.

— Hank Green (@hankgreen) March 25, 2017

All data comes from @realDonaldTrump via the Twitter API, with a few limitations on how many tweets this app can load because of the Twitter API.

This app, and its associated source code, are completely open source and available on GitHub for you to check out, edit, and examine for yourself.
